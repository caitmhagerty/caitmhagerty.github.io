<strong>Miso Salmon Recipe<br>

Courtesy of Foo Kitchens<br>

Prep time: 15 min<br>

Cooking time: 15 min<br>

Serves 5<br>

</strong>


<h2>Ingredients:</h2>

<ul>

<li>2 Tablespoons Yellow miso paste</li>

<li>2 Tablespoons Brown Sugar</li>

<li>2 teaspoons Soy sauce</li>

<li>2 Tablespoons Lemon juice</li>

<li>1 Tablespoon Mirin</li>

<li>1 Salmon filet, skin removed</li>

<li>1/2 cup Green onion, chopped</li>

</ul>


<h2>Preparation:</h2>

<ol>

<li>Create the glaze by whisking together the miso paste, brown sugar, soy sauce, lemon juice, and Mirin.</li>  

<li>Place the salmon filet on a roasting pan and coat the top with the glaze.</li> 

<li>Broil the filet for 10-15 minutes or until the filet is just cooked through. Do not overcook.</li>

<li>Remove the filet from the broiler and top with the green onions.</li>

<li>Serve with rice and stir-fried veggies.</li>

</ol>
